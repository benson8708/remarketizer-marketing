Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "minor edit"?

Minor edits include things like updating your hours, changing phone numbers, swapping out images, or small text changes. These are included in your monthly subscription at no extra charge.

What if I need a new page or a bigger change?

We call these "major revisions." They are billed at a flat rate of $50 per revision. You can request them easily through your customer dashboard, and we'll provide a quote before any work begins.

Do I own the website?

The website we build is part of our subscription service. As long as you are an active subscriber, you have full use of the site. If you cancel, the site is taken offline at the end of your billing period.

Why do you build the demo first?

We believe in showing, not just telling. By building the demo first, we prove the quality of our work and eliminate the risk for you. You get to see exactly what you're getting before you commit a single dollar.

What if I don't like the demo?

No problem at all. There is zero obligation. We only want you to become a customer if you are genuinely thrilled with the demo we've built for your business.
